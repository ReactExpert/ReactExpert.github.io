Getting Started

Mainstream solutions such as Paypal, Google Wallet, Cash.me, SquareCash and so forth all strictly forbid adult content & services with them. When you get caught, sanctions can be severe - for example, Paypal will ban your account, may refund some of your customers, and will freeze your funds for 6 months. Also, they often put your personal information at risk. We’ve built IndieBill especially for the independent adult entrepreneurs so that you have an hassle-free & anonymous way to process payments, without getting your revenues split in half by some site. With us, you can process credit card payments in a snap while getting 85% per sale. And we also go beyond payments, protecting you from chargebacks and automatically delivering sold content to your customers.

Due to the nature of our business, we ask you to upload a proof of identity (ID card, Passport or Driver License) before being able to process payments with us. Yet, we understand you want to remain anonymous. That is why we never share your personal info to third parties; and you can sign up to IndieBill with just an email, have a look around and decide whether or not to verify your identity later. As soon as you get your account validated (we review submitted documents within 72 hours, but in special cases it could take up to 1 week) you will be able to share your payment pages to your customers and start selling. If you need any help getting started, don’t hesitate to hit us up on the live chat !

Once your account has been validated, you will be able to start processing payments with us. To do so, you simply need to go over to the Products section and create payment pages for what you wish to sell with us. For example, if you sell Skype Shows then you would click “Sell a new Skype show”, fill out the form and get a payment page. Simply share its link to your customers for them to purchase, we’ll notify you by email as soon as they do. Here is a demo page for you to see. We also provide you with a store that features all the products you’ve decided, so that you can easily show your customers everything you offer on sale.
